Hi there! I’m Pragya.

I create digital projects and lead creative campaigns on anything: Politics, People & Places.

My work blends research, design, data and storytelling — from brand identity and websites to community-driven initiatives and workshops. You may find my work in Sumana Shrestha Secretariat, HowtoDeshBikas, Shethekranti, AfriSTEM & more.

I recently graduated with a degree in computer science (first-class honors), but much of what I've learned comes from experiences beyond the classroom. I've had the opportunity to work and learn in Cape Town, Pune, New York, and Kathmandu — where I grew up and call home — and each of these places has shaped how I see the world and approach my work.
